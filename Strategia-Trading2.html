üîß How the Indicator Works: Hierarchical Approach

The "4H Enhanced Strategy" utilizes a sophisticated 3-level hierarchical approach to filter and validate trading signals, integrating advanced filters and volume analysis for robust performance.

Hierarchical Level Component Function and Importance
LEVEL 1 - PRIMARY FILTER Linear Regression Position MANDATORY FILTER
Calculates the price position relative to the linear regression line. This is the first and most crucial filter.
-1 = Far below the line (extreme oversold)
+1 = Far above the line (extreme overbought)
Without a strong LinReg signal, NO valid trades.
Pearson R (Trend Quality) QUALITY VALIDATOR
Measures the strength of the linear correlation of the trend. Only with |R| ≥ 0.7 are signals considered reliable and tradable. This ensures you only trade in sufficiently strong trends.
Weak Pearson R (|R| < 0.7) = No Trading.
LEVEL 2 - CONFLUENCE FILTERS Bollinger Bands Position CONFIRMS OVERBOUGHT/OVERSOLD
Identifies when price is at extreme levels relative to its volatility. Contributes +1/-1 to the Confluence Score when extreme.
Stochastic RSI Enhanced MOMENTUM AND TIMING
Optimized version with %K=1 for higher reactivity. Detects anticipatory K/D crosses and extreme overbought/oversold zones (20/80 with buffer).
Pivot Points Filter SUPPORTS AND RESISTANCES
Identifies key reversal levels (support/resistance). Contributes to confluence when price is within a specified proximity (e.g., 2%) of these levels.
Advanced Moving Average Filter LONG-TERM TREND CONFIRMATION
Analyzes EMA 50 and SMA 200 for Golden Cross (bullish) and Death Cross (bearish) signals. Provides strong confirmation for the prevailing long-term trend.
Enhanced Volume Analysis SIGNAL VALIDATION & CONVICTION
Calculates the ratio of current volume to its moving average. High volume (>1.5x) on a signal adds strong conviction, while low volume warns of weak interest. Particularly important for confirming MA crosses.
SCORING SYSTEM Confluence Score INTELLIGENT AGGREGATOR
Sums contributions from all Level 2 secondary indicators. A score of ≥ +1 indicates Bullish Confluence, while ≤ -1 indicates Bearish Confluence. Only with sufficient confluence AND a primary LinReg signal is a trade considered valid.
